# Mina et le jardin des nuages — Image Flow

## Visual Direction

- Premium hand-painted gouache with layered cut-paper texture and visible paper grain.
- Mobile-first portrait compositions with a darker, calm lower zone for reader copy.
- Botanical green balanced with teal, coral, mustard, sky blue, terracotta, and charcoal.
- Natural educational details; no labels, in-image text, fantasy effects, logos, or watermarks.

## Character Lock

**Mina:** 7 years old, warm brown skin, round expressive face, large dark-brown eyes, black curly hair in two high puff buns with small teal ties. Teal short overalls, coral short-sleeve shirt, mustard-yellow rain boots, small red metal watering can.

**Grand-papa Youssef:** older North African man, light olive skin, short silver beard, round glasses, pale-blue rolled-sleeve shirt, charcoal trousers.

## Environment Lock

Urban Montreal rooftop garden with terracotta planters, tomato vines, basil, marigolds, a weathered wooden potting table, dark teal rain barrel, brick service wall, railing, and distant hilltop skyline. The dew collector uses low bamboo frames, angled clear panels, a narrow gutter, and glass jars.

## Scene Map

1. `01-thirsty-rooftop` — Mina discovers drooping plants while Youssef checks the empty barrel.
2. `02-three-drops` — Three drops remain; condensation appears on a cool glass.
3. `03-condensation` — Ice-jar experiment makes invisible vapor visible.
4. `04-the-plan` — Mina draws a wordless dew-collector plan.
5. `05-building-clouds` — Mina and Youssef assemble the low bamboo collector.
6. `06-night-watch` — Cool night air begins forming droplets on the panels.
7. `07-first-water` — Dawn droplets travel into a jar.
8. `08-every-drop` — Collected water goes to roots beneath protective mulch.
9. `09-garden-breathes` — The healthy rooftop garden closes the experiment.

## Generation Rule

Use scene 1 as the identity and environment reference for every later generation. Repeat the complete character lock, recurring rooftop objects, medium, palette, and exclusions in each prompt. Change only the action, time of day, framing, and educational focal point.
