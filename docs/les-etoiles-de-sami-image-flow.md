# Les étoiles de Sami — Image Flow

## Visual Direction

- Premium nocturnal watercolor on cold-press paper with visible granulation.
- Delicate colored-pencil linework and restrained screen-printed stars.
- Deep indigo and ultramarine balanced by rust, mint, cobalt, teal, mustard, and amber windows.
- Mobile-first portrait composition with calm dark space near the lower edge for reader copy.

## Character Lock

**Sami:** 8 years old, medium olive-brown skin, expressive oval face, thick black curly hair, dark eyes, round cobalt-blue glasses. Rust-orange zip hoodie, mint-green striped pajama shirt, navy joggers, white high-top sneakers, dark-blue star notebook.

**Tante Leïla:** woman in her mid-30s with warm olive skin, kind dark eyes, expressive eyebrows, deep-teal hijab, mustard-yellow cardigan, cream blouse, dark trousers, practical shoes, and a red-filter flashlight.

## Environment Lock

An apartment balcony and its safe shared rooftop terrace: sturdy black railing or masonry wall, potted herbs, striped cushion, rooftop greenhouse, closed utility shed, folding stools, layered city roofs, and warm windows below a broad night sky.

## Scene Map

1. `01-the-blinking-star` — Sami notices one low scintillating star.
2. `02-leila-joins` — Leïla brings a red flashlight and an observation method.
3. `03-star-and-planet` — They compare a twinkling star with a steady planet.
4. `04-warm-air-test` — Warm rising air makes the distant view shimmer more.
5. `05-bending-light` — Rippling water makes a stationary coin appear displaced.
6. `06-moving-atmosphere` — Sami connects bending light to moving atmospheric layers.
7. `07-lights-down` — Lower local glare reveals many faint stars.
8. `08-evidence` — Sami organizes the observations in a wordless notebook.
9. `09-next-question` — Dawn closes the answer and opens the next question.

## Generation Rule

Use scene 1 as the identity and medium reference for every generation. Repeat both character locks, the recurring rooftop geography, palette, medium, and exclusions. Keep every visual phenomenon scientifically plausible and avoid labels, fantasy effects, unsafe roof behavior, logos, and watermarks.
